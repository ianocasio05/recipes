Description

It's chicken and its fried.

Overview

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 10 mins
  • Total: 20 mins
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 4lbs Chicken
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Waaaay more seasonings for the epic kick
  • 4-5 Eggs
  • Flour
  • Vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Add seasonings into flour mixture and egg mixtures
  2. Dip chicken into dreg mixture
  3. Coat chicken in flour mixture
  4. Repeat one more time for more crispy chicken
  5. Heat oil over meadium heat.
  6. Fry until one side is golden brown, flip and repeat.
  7. Drain on paper towel and serve.
  8. Additionaly add honey as soon as chicken comes out :)
