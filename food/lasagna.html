Description

I don't need to describe lasagna dude.

Overview

  • Prep: 30 mins
  • Cook: 2hrs 30mins
  • Total: 3hrs
  • Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1lb Sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4lb Lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup Minced onion
  • 2 Cloves garlic, crushed
  • 28oz Can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6oz Tomato paste
  • 2 6.5oz Cans tomato sauce
  • 1/2 Cup water
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons Dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon Fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1.5 teaspoons Salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper
  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 16oz Ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4lb Mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. In an oven cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, italian seasoning 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and parsley. Cover and simmer for 1.5hr, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  4. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water. In a separate bowl combine ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  5. Spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce into baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles over sauce. Spread half of ricotta mixture ontop. Top with 1/3 mozzarella cheese. Spoon 1.5 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, sprinkle 1/4 cup Parmesan. Repeat layers, top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan. Cover with foil (spray with oil to prevent sticking).
  6. Bake in oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake additional 25 minutes.
